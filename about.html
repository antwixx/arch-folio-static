About BR Design

Excellence in architectural design for over 35 years

Our Story

Founded with a vision to create exceptional architectural solutions, BR Design & Architecture has been serving clients in the Chicago area for over 35 years. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction.

From our earliest residential projects to today's complex commercial and healthcare facilities, we've maintained our core philosophy: architecture should be functional, beautiful, and sustainable. Every project we undertake reflects our dedication to these principles.

Our team brings together diverse expertise in residential, commercial, and healthcare architecture. We believe that great design emerges from understanding our clients' needs, respecting the environment, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
